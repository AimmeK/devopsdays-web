*** COVID-19 UPDATE ***

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we the organizers of DevOpsDays DFW had decided that in the community's safety and well-being best interest not to proceed with our event on August 26-27, 2021 as planned. We have it re-scheduled to occur on August 24-25, 2022. We are constantly monitoring the data provided by our health experts at UT Southwestern Medical Center for the North Texas, and DFW area.

In the month of January 2022, we will be discussing the CDC Guidelines with the Plano Event Center to decide if it will be safe for our community to proceed with re-scheduled dates in August 2022 or postpone it further.

If you have any questions/concerns, feel free to email us at: dallas@devopsdays.org .





The same group of seasoned organizers that brought you DevOpsDays DFW several years in a row are back again another year in 2022 to celebrate our 5th Anniversary. We were encouraged and motivated by the feedback received from previous year's events, which is why they are determined to bring an even better experience this year and improve the DevOps conversation throughout the DFW Metroplex.
