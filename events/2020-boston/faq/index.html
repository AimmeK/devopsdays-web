1. Is DevOpsDays Boston Still Happening?

Yes! DevOpsDays Boston 2020 is still happening. All content and programming will take place online.

2. When is DevOpsDays Boston?

The keynote, talks and breakout sessions will take place virtually on Tuesday, September 29th. Stay tuned for updates to our agenda.

3. When will the schedule be available?

The finalized agenda will be published here. Please check back for updates, as they will be provided on an ongoing basis.

4. Where is DevOpsDays Boston?

Join us from your mobile device or laptop. In alignment with best practices laid out by the CDC and WHO, the large scale in-person component in Boston has been cancelled.

5. How do I get into the event?

If you’ve already registered, click the link you were emailed by boston@devopsdays.org. If you haven’t registered for the event yet, you can do so here.

6. Where are the proceeds donated?

All proceeds will be donated to nonprofits supporting underrepresented communities in tech.

7. When do doors open?

Our virtual “doors” will open to attendees at 9 AM EDT / 1PM GMT on September 29th and will remain open until the following day to accommodate attendees located across the world.

8. Can my company sponsor DevOpsDays Boston?

If you’re interested in sponsoring DevOpsDays Boston, please check out our Sponsor FAQ.

9. What is your code of conduct?

We want everyone at DevOpsDays Boston to have a great time! Please help us out by following our Code of Conduct.
