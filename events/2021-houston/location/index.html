devopsdays Houston - Location

COVID-19

The onference will take place live and in-person. The Norris Conference Centers have taken measures to make their venue as safe as possible, and we at DevOpsDays Houston take safety seriously. We will monitor and adhere to local guidelines on handling of the Covid19 virus. Stay tuned to our Twitter feed or our LinkedIn page for conference news and any changes.

Address

The conference will take place at the Norris Conference Centers, Houston/CityCenter.

816 Town and Country Blvd #210
Houston, TX 77024

Parking

For your convenience, we encourage you to park in the garage that is attached to the Norris Centers location (paralleling Beltway 8). Please park on the 3rd level of the parking garage.

CityCenter has several other parking garages, with free parking available on the upper levels of garages.
