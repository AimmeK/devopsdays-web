devopsdays Tel Aviv - Location

Conference Location


DevOpsDays Tel Aviv will be held at Expo Tel Aviv, Pavilion 10


Expo Tel Aviv
101 Rokach Blvd,
Tel Aviv-Yafo

The Convention Center is located right next to the beautiful Yarkon Park and offers easy transport options and accessibility - bus, train, taxi, bicycle, and even by foot.

Transportation:


Bus

If you are coming by bus, have a look at the Moovit app for information on the best route here.


Train

​If you are coming by train you can check schedules at the Israel Railways website.  You will need to get off at the Tel Aviv University station.


Bicycle

There are bicycle rental points all around Tel Aviv, appropriately called Tel-O-Fun, you can find all of the bike rental spots and the app for download on the website.

Taxi & Ride Sharing

There are numerous ride-sharing and taxi apps in Tel Aviv, you can try Gett, Uber, Yango, and even Bubble for adaptive route ride-sharing.

Find more info about the conference here.



Parking

For participants coming by car, parking is available within the Convention Center grounds. The cost to participants is 29 shekels for the day.


Accommodation

Tel Aviv is home to Israel’s most prestigious beachfront hotels. The main stretch of hotels is right across from the Tel Aviv Marina. You can find more information about the location of the various hotels here. ​

Leisure

Tel Aviv is one of the most culturally diverse cities you will ever see. There is a multitude of sights, tours, and museums that visitors can enjoy during their stay in Israel. ​ For more information on things to do in Tel Aviv, see here.
