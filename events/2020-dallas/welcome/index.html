*** COVID-19 UPDATE ***

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we the organizers of DevOpsDays DFW have decided that in the best interest of the community's safety and well-being not to proceed with our event on August 26-27, 2020 as planned. We have scheduled DevOpsDays Dallas 2021 to occur on August 25-26, 2021. If you have any questions/concerns, feel free to email us at: dallas@devopsdays.org .
