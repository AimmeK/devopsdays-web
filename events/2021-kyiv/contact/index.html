Yurii Rochniak

Organizer

N26

Yurii Rochniak is currently an SRE at N26 neobank where he’s working on creating an internal self-service platform for engineers. Through his career, he was focusing on infra development and CI/CD topics in the companies of various sizes. Yurii is a creator and administrator of CatOps Telegram channel with more than 5k subscribers and an initiator of HashiCorp User Group Kyiv. Yurii is originally from Kyiv, but currently located in Berlin. He’s into biking and stand up paddleboarding, when the weather allows it.
